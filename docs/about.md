# About Business Systems Engineering

Business Systems Engineering (**BSE**) is an Egyptian software development company founded in **2007** and headquartered in Giza. We specialize in building **integrated ERP systems**, **e-invoicing** solutions, and **custom software** for organizations across Egypt and the broader MENA region.

Our public website: [**bse.com.eg**](https://bse.com.eg/?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=docs-site&utm_campaign=bse-documentation&utm_content=about)

## Vision

Information systems are one of the most important investments a business can make. When designed well, they provide the information and tools organizations need to grow — maximizing the use of resources and reducing waste.

## Mission

To be one of the top Egyptian software companies supporting the development of the private sector and its ability to contribute to sustainable economic growth.

## Why customers choose BSE

- **Fully integrated** business solutions — not a patchwork of disconnected modules.
- **Deep customization** — our platforms are ~95 % customizable to fit your actual workflows.
- **Long-term partnership** — customer service and post-sale support are core to how we operate, not an afterthought.
- **Arabic-first, bilingual** — we build and support in both Arabic and English.
- **Tax-Invoice ready** — every BSE ERP ships with Egyptian e-invoicing support out of the box.
